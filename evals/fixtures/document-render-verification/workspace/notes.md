# Proposal Notes: Atlas Onboarding Revamp

Client: Meridian Logistics (ops team, ~200 seats)

Problem observed in discovery calls:
- New dispatchers take 3+ weeks to reach full speed.
- Most errors happen in manual carrier assignment during week 1-2.
- Current training is a 90-page PDF nobody finishes.

Proposed approach:
- Guided first-run flow inside the dispatch screen.
- Scenario-based practice mode with the client's own historical orders.
- Weekly usage report to team leads for the first 8 weeks.

Timeline sketch:
- Weeks 1-2 discovery and content mapping
- Weeks 3-6 build guided flow and practice mode
- Weeks 7-8 pilot with one regional team, then rollout

Team: 1 PM (part-time), 2 engineers, 1 instructional designer.

Open items from our side:
- Pricing not agreed internally yet.
- Success metric baseline needs client data access.
